## Connecting Your Wallet

### Step 1: Click Connect

Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner.

### Step 2: Select Wallet

Choose your wallet provider:

- MetaMask
- WalletConnect
- Coinbase Wallet

### Step 3: Sign Message

Sign the authentication message to create your session.

